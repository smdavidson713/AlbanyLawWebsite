Direct Lobbying Social Media Test

The information in this module is meant to help you determine if your organization engages in direct lobbying through social media. Because lobbying activity can take place through social media channels, it is important that this activity is not overlooked. Watching the video below can help you determine if your organization's online activity is considered lobbying. If you conclude that you engage in acts that might be considered Direct Lobbying Social Media activity, then it is likely you will have to report this activity as lobbying to state authorities. Consult with an attorney if you feel you need guidance on whether you need to report such activity and on how to do so if necessary.
